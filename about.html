    • orpheus is an introspective, interactive web program.

    a mix of digital art animation, creative writing, and html.

    orpheus hopes to invoke self-reflection through an inward-looking narrative. take a journey and learn something about yourself.

    pause in between pages. write down your thoughts. this is meant to provoke introspection and stimulate mindfulness.

    please use full screen on desktop for the fullest experience. unfortunately, orpheus does not work on mobile. you should also allow autoplay on your browser for this site to enable the background music.

    i hope you enjoy.
